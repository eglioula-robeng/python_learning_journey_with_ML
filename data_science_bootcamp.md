# Business Intelligence (BI)

Business Intelligence is the process of analyzing and reporting historical business data. The aim is to explain past events using business data. Because it focuses on historical analysis, BI can be considered an intersection between Business Analytics and Data Science, serving as a preliminary step toward predictive analytics.

1) Analyze past data and extract useful insights
2) Create appropriate models for decision-making and forecasting

# Machine Learning

Machine Learning is the ability of machines to predict outcomes without being explicity programmed. It is about creating and implementing algorithms that let machines receive data and use this data to:

1) make predictions
2) analyse patterns
3) give recommendations on their own


# Artifical Intelligence 

Artifical Intelligence simulates human knowledge and decision making with computers.
